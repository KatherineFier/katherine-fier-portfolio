Boulder Flower Farm

Case Study

Overview

The Boulder Flower Farm is owned by The Fresh Herb Company, an established wholesale flower and herb farm. The owners wanted a new reoccuring revenue stream that required little to no physical labor. This new revenue stream became the Boulder Flower Farm, a high-end wedding venue business. They needed a new brand, website and marketing strategy.

This project included 5 phases: Research and Branding, Website Build, Marketing Strategy and Implementation, and Customer Experience.
