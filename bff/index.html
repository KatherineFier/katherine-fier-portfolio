Case Study:
The Boulder Flower Farm

Overview

The founders of an established wholesale flower farm located in Boulder County, CO wanted a new reccuring revenue stream. The revenue stream would be an outdoor event venue business. A new brand, website and marketing strategy was needed.

Process

This project included 5 phases: Brand Research, Visual Design, Website Build, Marketing Strategy and Implementation, and Customer Experience.

Phase I: Brand Research

  • identified 5 unique selling points
  • identified ideal customer
  • created brand name, secured domain names and social handles

Phase II: Visual Design

  • chose brand colors & fonts
  • designed logo, favicon, and style guide
  • created business cards & fliers

Phase III: Web Design

  • built custom 14 page mobile responsive website with a 6% conversion rate
  • wrote website copy
  • integrated SEO best practices (on-page and off-page)

Phase IV: Digital Marketing

  • established 30+ brand partnerships
  • earned national & regional media coverage
  • developed engaged social media following

Phase V: Customer Experience

  • designed label and packaging for three new physical products
  • streamlined and documented customer service and marketing processes
  • solicited over a dozen 4.9 star client reviews
Work With Me

Challenge

The primary challenge in this project was solidifying stakeholder buy in to engage in digital marketing. To overcome this challenge, I proposed that I start small with digital marketing and track the results. I presented the initial results/ROI to the stakeholders, enabling them to see the value of this strategy. I then optimized the initial strategy to zero in on where we saw the most results (SEO and B2B partnerships developed via our social media presence).

Results

  • Developed a new brand (The Boulder Flower Farm)
  • Grew new line of revenue, increasing parent company's yearly business profitability by 25%
  • Fully booked out the venue, the first and second season post-Covid
  • Established a 6% website conversion rate
