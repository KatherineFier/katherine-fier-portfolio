Case Study:
The Boulder Flower Farm

Overview

The founders of an established wholesale flower and herb farm located in Boulder County, CO wanted a new reccuring revenue stream. The new revenue stream would be an outdoor wedding venue business. A new brand, website and marketing strategy was needed.

The Process

This project included 5 phases: Research and Branding, Visual Design, Website Build, Marketing Strategy and Implementation, and Customer Experience.

Work With Me

Results

Developed a new brand (The Boulder Flower Farm) and grew a new line of revenue, increasing the parent company's overall yearly business profitability by 20%. Fully booked out the venue, the first and second season post-Covid. Established a 6% website conversion rate.

Phase I: Brand Research

  • identified 5 unique selling points
  • identified ideal customer
  • created brand name, secured domain names and social handles

Phase II: Visual Design

  • chose brand colors & fonts
  • designed logo & favicon, and style guide
  • created business cards & fliers

Phase III: Web Design

  • built custom 14 page mobile responsive website with a 6% conversion rate
  • wrote website copy
  • integrated SEO best practices (direct search became highest source of traffic)

Phase IV: Digital Marketing

  • established 30+ brand partnerships
  • earned national & regional media coverage
  • developed engaged social media following

Phase V: Customer Experience

  • designed label and packaging for three new physical products
  • streamlined and documented customer service and marketing processes
  • solicited over a dozen 4.9 star client reviews
